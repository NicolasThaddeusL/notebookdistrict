About Us

Who are We?

We are a group of students who decided to help create a sustainable school environment
by selling premium high-quality items at a reasonable price for other students.

What are Our Goals?

We aim to achieve an environment where students can prepare for their studies
using quality-ensured items without a high price.

What is Our Story?

Notebook District started as a small group project at the end of a high school year. It emerged as one of the victors in a business idea competition.
However, it wasn't all sunshine and rainbows, as we hit a roadblock. "How will we gain a profit when selling these supplies?", our founder thought.
As time went on, our numbers dwindled. It was a disaster.
Only our founder and our supervisor stayed loyal. We were about to shutdown, until...
We found it! We would only make a small profit each time, but that's what mattered.
We regathered our old team, and finally started this company into a nationwide supplier!

What are Our Values?

Integrity.
Trust.
On-Time.
Quality Ensured.
